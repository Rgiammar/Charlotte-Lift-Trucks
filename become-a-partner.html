The Process
How It Works

A simple four-step process from lead generation to handoff. No complicated onboarding, no long-term contracts to start.

01

We Run the Ads

Charlotte Lift Trucks runs targeted Google Ads and organic SEO to capture businesses in the Charlotte metro actively searching for forklift sales, rentals, and leases.

02

We Vet the Lead

Every submission goes through our matching desk. We verify it's a legitimate business with a real equipment need before it ever reaches you.

03

We Send It to You

You receive a lead email with the customer's full contact information, equipment need, timeline, and any additional details they provided.

04

You Close the Deal

You contact the customer directly and handle the transaction your way. We stay out of it. A flat referral fee is due at the time we deliver the lead.

Ideal Partners
Who We're Looking For

We work best with independent local forklift providers who have the inventory, bandwidth, and responsiveness to follow up on leads quickly and serve Charlotte area customers well.

  • Independent forklift dealers, rental companies, or leasing operations
  • Serving the Charlotte metro and surrounding counties
  • Able to respond to leads promptly -- customers move fast
  • Offering at least one of: sales, rental, or leasing
  • Licensed and in good standing in your state of operation
  • Committed to a professional customer experience

Not the Right Fit If...

You are a national chain or large regional operation with a centralized call center -- our model is built around independent local providers who give customers a direct, personal experience.

You cannot respond to leads within a reasonable timeframe -- leads go cold fast and we want to match customers with providers who will follow up the same day.
